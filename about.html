About Us

Drews Food Limited is the sister company to C.A Uchegbu Ventures, an established player in the Nigerian biscuit distribution market for the past couple of years both owned by the same founder now making a transformative leap into manufacturing. Having successfully solidified our presence as a leading distributor, we strategically expanded our operations by setting up a state-of-the-art biscuit manufacturing factory in Aba, Abia State, Nigeria. This evolution represents an exciting phase in our journey as we capitalize on our distribution success and market knowledge to revolutionize the biscuit production landscape. Company Background: Having demonstrated its expertise in the biscuit distribution sector by securing a strong foothold as a major distributor for Yale Foods, Pardee foods. Drews Food Limited resumed it's operations in biscuit manufacturing. Our strategic alliances and meticulous distribution network through our sister company C.A Uchegbu Ventures has enabled us to command a significant market share in Eastern Nigeria, attaining the trust of both consumers and partners. Building on these accomplishments, we are excited we took the next step and expanded into manufacturing.
